Welcome to the World of K-On!

K-On! is a heartwarming anime by Kyoto Animation, based on Kakifly's manga. It follows a group of high school girls who form a band in their light music club. Their journey is filled with fun, friendship, and, of course, Mugi’s delicious tea parties!

Main Characters

Yui Hirasawa
Carefree lead guitarist

Mio Akiyama
Shy bassist & songwriter

Ritsu Tainaka
Energetic drummer

Tsumugi Kotobuki
Tea-loving keyboardist

Azusa Nakano
Diligent junior guitarist

Mugi’s Famous Tea Parties

Mugi’s tea parties are a core part of the club! The girls love to enjoy tea, biscuits, and sweets before practice. Here are some of their favorites:

  • Black Tea (Earl Grey)
  • Shortbread & Butter Cookies
  • All sorts of Cookies! They really love Cookies!
